About

We’re a three-piece cover band from Northern Virginia, comprised of Jeff Sciscilo on lead-guitar/keys, Bryan Hadro on rhythm guitar, and Hugh O’Donnell on bass. We've been playing music together since college (nearly 25 years) and specialize in playing music from the 50’s, 60’s and 70’s, with particular focus on vocal harmonies. We love playing local wineries, breweries and events in the area. Follow us on Facebook and YouTube for upcoming shows and updates!
